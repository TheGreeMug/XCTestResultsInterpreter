Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

This policy describes how we handle information in relation to the XCResult Report Generator and MP3 Metadata Stripper tools (the “Services”) in accordance with applicable data protection law, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) where it applies.

1. Who is responsible for your data (data controller)

The data controller in respect of the Services is the operator of this website (the same entity that makes the Services available to you). For the purpose of exercising your rights or for any privacy-related question, please use the contact means provided on the main site or at office@assurance.st

2. What data we process and why (purposes and legal basis)

Uploaded files. When you use the Services, you upload files (e.g. .zip bundles or MP3 files). These are processed on our servers solely to provide the service you request (generating a report or returning a stripped audio file). We do not retain your files after processing. Temporary copies are deleted as soon as the request is completed, or after a short retention period for report jobs (typically up to 30 minutes), after which they are permanently removed. We do not archive, analyse, or use the content of your uploads for any other purpose.

Legal basis (GDPR): Processing of your uploaded files is necessary for the performance of the contract between you and us (i.e. to provide the service you request). We do not rely on your consent for this processing. Because we do not retain your files, the data are not stored beyond the time strictly necessary to fulfil the request.

Technical data. When you access the site or call the API, our servers (and any infrastructure or hosting providers we use) may automatically receive technical data such as your IP address, browser type, and request metadata. We do not use this data to identify you or to build profiles. We do not use cookies or similar technologies for tracking. We do not sell your data to anyone.

Legal basis (GDPR): Such technical processing may be necessary for our legitimate interest in operating and securing the Services, or for compliance with a legal obligation, to the extent applicable. Where required by law, we will rely on the appropriate legal basis and inform you.

3. No account or identity required

We do not require you to create an account or to provide your name, email address, or other identifying information to use the Services. Access may be protected by an API key that you or your organisation configure; we do not use that key to identify or profile you.

4. Retention

We do not retain your uploaded files. Temporary copies are deleted after processing (or after the short retention window for report jobs, as described above). We do not maintain an ongoing dataset of your personal data or file content. If we keep technical or access logs for operational or security purposes, we retain them only for as long as necessary and in accordance with applicable law.

5. Your rights (including under the GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, or if your jurisdiction grants similar rights, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data we hold (subject to applicable exceptions)
  • Rectification of inaccurate personal data
  • Erasure (“right to be forgotten”) in certain circumstances
  • Restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Data portability, where the processing is carried out by automated means and is based on contract or consent
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests, and to processing for direct marketing
  • Withdraw consent at any time, where processing is based on consent, without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority in your country (e.g. in the EU, the data protection authority of your member state)

Because we do not retain your uploaded files or build profiles, many of these rights will have limited practical application; we will still respond to any valid request in accordance with the law. To exercise your rights or for any privacy-related question, please contact us using the means provided on the main site or in the Service interface.

If any of your data is processed outside the European Economic Area or the UK, we will ensure that appropriate safeguards (such as adequacy decisions, standard contractual clauses, or other mechanisms approved by the relevant authorities) are in place where required by law.

7. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data against unauthorised access, loss, or alteration, in line with the nature of the data and the risks involved. Because we do not retain your files, the window of exposure is limited to the time necessary for processing.

8. Automated decision-making and profiling

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling within the meaning of the GDPR.

9. Minors

The Services are not directed at minors. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have processed data relating to a child, please contact us so we can address it.
